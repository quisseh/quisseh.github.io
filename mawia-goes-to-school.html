mawia goes to school!

A bite-sized top-down exploration game with an agenda; a proposal in disguise

  • Download
    ZIP File
  • Donate To quisseh

Experience a not-so-average day in the life of a schoolteacher, mawia.

  • Get her ready for work without tripping over her GameCube.
  • Ignore the bearded man hiding in her hallway closet.
  • Navigate highway traffic without crashing into every passing car.
  • Quiz her students on whale anatomy.
  • Oh, and help her decide who she should spend the rest of her life with.

System requirements
