air-blue dream

A dreamy vertical platformer

You are a flightless bird on an upward climb through forested foothills.

Choose between a stroll…

  • go your own pace through increasingly difficult terrain
  • frequent run-ins with the forest's inhabitants, whose faces look suspiciously like yours
  • you can't die

or a sprint.

  • traverse a random path at high speed
  • the forest is uninhabited
  • you will die

Keep in mind the path ahead may be behind you.

Keyboard controls

  • Run: Left/Right, A/D
  • Jump: Shift, Ctrl, Space, Z, X, C, Q, E
  • Crouch: Down, S
  • Enter a portal: Up, W

Gamepad controls

  • Run: D-pad left/D-pad right
  • Jump: A, B, X, Y
  • Crouch: D-pad down
  • Enter a portal: D-pad up

System requirements

  • Windows XP and up
